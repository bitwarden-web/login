Privacy Policy

Your digital sanctuary deserves transparent protection. This policy outlines how Warden-web safeguards your privacy through design principles that honor your trust.

Data Collection and Processing

Our digital sanctuary operates on privacy by design principles. We collect only essential data required for service functionality:

  • Account credentials (encrypted at rest)
  • Service usage patterns for security monitoring
  • Technical logs for system maintenance
  • Communication preferences for service updates

All personal data undergoes encryption before storage. Our invisible armor extends to every byte of information entrusted to our systems.

Cookies and Tracking

Our encrypted presence utilizes minimal tracking mechanisms:

Essential Cookies

Required for authentication and security. These cannot be disabled without compromising service functionality.

Analytics Cookies

Anonymous usage statistics to improve service quality. These can be disabled through your browser settings.

We do not employ third-party tracking scripts or behavioral profiling systems. Your digital movements remain your own.

Analytics and Monitoring

Our silent protection includes security monitoring systems that:

  • Detect unauthorized access attempts
  • Monitor system performance and availability
  • Analyze traffic patterns for security threats
  • Generate anonymized usage reports

All monitoring data is processed locally within our infrastructure. No external analytics services access your behavioral patterns.

Your Rights Under GDPR

As a guardian of your digital sanctuary, you possess these fundamental rights:

Right to Access

Request copies of your personal data

Right to Rectification

Correct inaccurate personal information

Right to Erasure

Request deletion of your data

Right to Portability

Transfer your data to another service

Right to Restrict

Limit how we process your data

Right to Object

Oppose certain data processing

Data Security Measures

Our invisible armor employs multiple layers of protection:

  • End-to-end encryption for all data transmission
  • AES-256 encryption for data at rest
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Zero-knowledge architecture where possible
  • Secure data centers with physical access controls
  • Regular backup systems with encrypted storage

We maintain security incident response procedures and will notify affected users within 72 hours of any data breach discovery.

Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary for service provision:

  • Active account data: Duration of service usage
  • Security logs: 90 days maximum
  • Communication records: 2 years for support purposes
  • Deleted account data: 30 days for recovery, then permanent deletion

All data destruction follows secure deletion protocols ensuring complete removal from our systems.

Third-Party Services

Our digital sanctuary minimizes external dependencies. When third-party services are necessary:

  • All providers undergo privacy impact assessments
  • Data processing agreements ensure GDPR compliance
  • Regular audits verify continued compliance
  • Data minimization principles apply to all transfers

We maintain a current list of all data processors and their purposes upon request.
